Game Introduction

Hello!! Welcome to my first shooting game, this game is reference from the HTML5 Game Tutorial on YouTube,the URL is https://youtu.be/eI9idPTT0c4

When you click the start button,you can move youself by pressing 'A' 'W' 'D' 'S', you can also click and shoot bullets,and when your bullet hits or kills the enemy, you will get the golds, these golds can be used to upgrade the skills, and Reset Skill button can reset your skill,and keeps your previous golds . There are 60 levels in this game,and after every level 20, there will be new enemies that are stronger than the previous enemies, and you also will get more golds, your goal is survive to 30 seconds, when time's up, you can go to the next level, although you hava broken the level, you also can enter the previous levels, these previous level can not be countdown.When you break the every level 20, you will get the reward. By the way,this game can not be saved, so if you press 'F5', your data will be gone,and if you want to look code which I wrote, you can press 'Ctrl + U' or go to my Github:https://github.com/JiaLong0209/playground/tree/main/shooting-game This is my game introducion, hope you have fun.
